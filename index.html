Our Story

Welcome

PERSONAL REPOSITORY MANAGER is a project of the x86, a nonprofit organization dedicated to the development of software and services for researchers and cultural heritage institutions.

This project was developed as part of SE course V/I.

OUR FEATURES

Why Personal Repo Manager?

It is a free, easy-to-use tool to help you collect, organize, cite, and share research.
Collect and Organize

Import a bibtex or add journals, conferences and books manually.
Organize your research any way you want.
Automatically fill with relevant materials as you work.

All within a few clicks

Cite and Share

Create references and bibliographies for any text editor, and directly inside Word, LibreOffice, and Google Docs.
Cite your sources in proper format
Share yuor papers among friends, colleagues and others.

Supports MLA and APA format(also Excel)

Collaborate

Co-write a paper with a colleague, distribute course materials to students, or build a collaborative bibliography.
Share your library with as many people you like, at no cost.
